About Me

I build things that feel real — whether that's a VR heist game where you grab jewels with your bare hands, or this portfolio that simulates a full Fedora 43 desktop in the browser. I got hooked on VR after implementing Meta Quest hand tracking and realising gesture input could replace controllers entirely. When I'm not in Unity, I'm usually deep in vanilla JS, Linux configs, or figuring out how to make CSS do something it probably shouldn't.

How I Got Here

My journey into technology began with curiosity about how digital systems work and a desire to create solutions that matter. After completing high school, I knew I wanted to pursue a career that combines logical thinking with creative problem-solving, leading me to Information Technology.

At Singapore Polytechnic, I'm developing skills in software development, database management, and responsive web design. The precision required in programming appeals to me – every line of code matters, structure follows logical patterns, and the difference between good and great software often lies in the details most users never see.

Currently, I balance my studies with part-time work at Capella Hotel, where I've learned valuable communication and interpersonal skills. This experience has taught me the importance of user experience and customer service – principles I apply to my approach to software development and technology solutions.

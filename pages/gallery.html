I rarely used to take photos, mainly because I wasn't professional at that and most of my old photos also proved that correct. But one time I found the photo album that my mom kept where all my childhood memories are attached and most of them I barely remember. Smart phones and cameras are no where to be found at that time but my parents did their best to make sure I had a core memory. I really thankful to them for that and it is my privilege to do the same in my turn.
